Pick & Bring Leuven

How to use Pick & Bring services?

Step 1: Place Your Order Online

- Visit the website of your desired shop.
- Choose either Pickup or Takeout for your order.
- Complete the payment process directly on the shop's website.

Step 2: Submit Your Delivery Request

- Open the Pick & Bring map.
- Click exactly on the shop's location from which you'd like your order to be picked up.
- Enter the shop's name, your order details, and your delivery address.
- Click "Submit" to proceed.

Step 3: Connect with the Delivery Driver

- You will receive an Order Code upon submission.
- Click "Send Code" to connect with your Delivery Driver.
- Once the code is sent, you'll be connected directly to the Delivery Driver.

Pick & Bring Advance Credit Service

How It Works:

1. Submit Your Shopping Request

- Open the Pick & Bring map and select your desired shop.
- Enter the shop's name and provide detailed shopping instructions, including the items you need.
- Add your delivery address and click "Submit."

2. Send the Order Code

- After submission, Click "Send Code" to connect with your Delivery Driver.
- The Delivery Driver will purchase your items and bring them to your address, along with the payment receipt.

3. Payment & Delivery

- The Advance Credit Limit is €20, so your shopping budget must be within this amount.
- The Delivery Driver will notify you of their arrival through WhatsApp.
- Pay the receipt amount plus the delivery fee upon delivery.

Add to Home Screen

- For quick and easy access, add Pick & Bring to your home screen to use it as an app.

Pay at delivery:

The Pick & Bring fee is 5€ per order.

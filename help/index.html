Pick & Bring Leuven

1. How to use Pick & Bring services?

Connect with Pick & Bring assistant

- Open the Pick & Bring website.
- Click on the map in the center, a chatbox will appear, here you can directly chat with Pick & Bring assistant.
- A unique User ID (XXXX) will be generated for your chat box.
- You can always re-open your previous chat by searching your User ID XXXX. So don't forget to note down Your User ID for future chat.
- Talk to the assistant and feel free to arrange your personal delivery & tasks.
- Assistant can do various pick & bring tasks.
- Assistant can even make a purchase on your behalf under 20 Euros.

Order from Google Maps Or Apple Maps:

- Click on your desired shop on Google Maps.
- Click the share button.
- Copy the link of the place.
- Send it to the rider in chat.
- Rider will take control of your Delivery.

4. How to Pay?

- Ensure the payment is done at your doorstep.
- You can pay via Cash, or Contactless payment with VISA, Mastercard etc.
- If you face any issues, just ask the rider through chat.

5. Add to Home Screen

- For quick and easy access, add Pick & Bring to your home screen to use it as an app.

6. Delivery fee is 5€ Only!

- The delivery fee for using the Pick & Bring service is just 5€.

7. Do you have any other question?

- You can simply ask your question to the rider in the chat.

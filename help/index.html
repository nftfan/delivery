Pick & Bring Leuven

1. How to use Pick & Bring services?

Step 1: Place Your Order Online

- Visit the website of your desired shop.
- Choose either Pickup or Takeout for your order.
- Complete the payment process directly on the shop's website.

Step 2: Connect with a Delivery Driver

- Open the Pick & Bring map.
- Touch the shop's location where you'd like your order to be picked up.
- Instantly connect with a Delivery Driver via the built-in chat feature.
- Discuss your order details directly with the driver.

2. Pick & Bring Advance Credit Service

How It Works:

1. Submit Your Shopping Request

- Open the Pick & Bring map and select your desired shop.
- Enter your shopping instructions through the chat.
- Provide your delivery address to the driver.

2. Payment & Delivery

- The Advance Credit Limit is €20, so your shopping budget must be within this amount.
- The Delivery Driver will notify you of their arrival through the chat.
- Pay the receipt amount plus the delivery fee upon delivery.
- Click the icon on the top left side of the map to pay.

Add to Home Screen

- For quick and easy access, add Pick & Bring to your home screen to use it as an app.

Delivery fee is 5€ Only!

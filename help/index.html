Pick & Bring Leuven

About Us

Pick & Bring is a personalized service platform designed to simplify your daily tasks and shopping from the shops in Leuven. Whether you need assistance with a quick purchase or other everyday services, our friendly assistants are just a chat away. You can start a conversation at any time, even if you’re not placing an order, and our assistants will gladly help with your requests.

For shopping services, we offer an advance credit limit of up to €20 (excluding the delivery fee). This means you can request items worth up to €20 from within Leuven, and we’ll deliver them right to your doorstep. You'll only need to pay the total receipt amount plus a small delivery fee upon arrival.

For other personal services, you can easily check the service fees by clicking the corresponding icons on our interactive map.

At Pick & Bring, we’re here to make life easier, whatever you need, we bring it to you!

FAQs

1. What is Pick & Bring?

Pick & Bring is a personal service platform that helps you manage everyday tasks and errands. You can request various services, from shopping to other personal needs, and our assistants will handle the rest.

2. How do I get started with Pick & Bring?

To start using our services, simply open the Pick & Bring website and initiate a chat with our assistant. You can ask any questions or request services directly through the chat interface.

3. What is the credit limit for shopping services?

The advance credit limit for shopping services is €20, excluding the delivery fee. This means you can request items worth up to €20 from Leuven, and we’ll deliver them to your doorstep. You can increase this limit to €50 on your third order.

4. How do I pay for the services?

Payment is made upon delivery. You’ll pay the total amount of the receipt plus the delivery fee. We accept cash and contactless payments such as VISA and Mastercard.

5. Can I see the prices for other services?

Yes, prices for other services are displayed on the interactive map. Simply click the icon of the service you’re interested in to view its price.

6. What if I have additional questions or requests?

You can ask any additional questions or make further requests by chatting with our assistant. They are available to assist you with any needs you may have.

7. How do I add Pick & Bring to my home screen?

For quick and easy access, you can add Pick & Bring to your home screen. This allows you to use our platform as a convenient app on your mobile device.

8. Is there a delivery fee?

Yes, there is a delivery fee of €5 for using the Pick & Bring service, in addition to the cost of your purchase.

We pick & bring your orders.
